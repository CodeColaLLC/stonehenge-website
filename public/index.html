What is Stonehenge?

Stonehenge is an extensible, modular platform that builds websites, intranets, and dashboards. Its intuitive interface makes it trivial to stack different kinds of components together to form beautiful layouts.

The focus is on the end experience – simplifying content management. Stonehenge is a framework for assembling many different interlocking components into a unified system. It’s designed from the ground up around consolidating your favorite widgets and applications.

Stonehenge is also equipped with a beautiful WYSIWYG editor. See for yourself by clicking the icon.

What Makes It Different?

  • An intuitive system to design screens using a row layout. Arranging widgets on your page is as easy as adding another row to your screen and choosing an arrangement of columns.
  • Not just content management – content control. Widgets in Stonehenge are tracked by version control, meaning that a complete history of the widget is stored and can be compared against or rolled back at any time.
  • A library of widgets to suit every need. Whether you need to just enter a paragraph of text, insert a Google Map, or display a searchable directory of your employees.
  • Growing your platform is how we grow ours. Your business is our business and your success is our growth. We don’t follow the generic SaaS model – each client’s website has different needs and requirements, and as your needs evolve, we can work with you to build proprietary widgets that adapt to your needs.
